The Lady Chapel

Location: east apse of Rosslyn. Richest carvings: angels, vines, Marian symbolism. Meaning: Sophia, Shekinah, flowering gnosis.

Intention

To honor the Lady Chapel as locus of flowering gnosis: Marian wisdom, Shekinah presence, angelic choir.

Technique

Accessible folio; schematic SVG of apse; Marian gold-lapis palette; museum style.

Lineage

  • Rosslyn east apse; Marian dedication.
  • Angel choir, vine motifs, biblical scenes.
  • Esoteric: Shekinah (Qabalah), Sophia (Gnostic), Magdalene devotion (interpretation).

Evidence

Carvings: angels, foliage, biblical reliefs. Antiquarian records describe the Lady Chapel as most richly decorated.

Reflection

The Lady Chapel blossoms with gnosis: divine feminine, flowering vine, angelic praise. A chamber of beauty, integration, and sacred presence.
