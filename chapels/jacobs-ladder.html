What this page is (and isn’t)

This is a study visualization of a traditional mystical ladder, aligned with museum standards. It is not a claim about a specific 33‑rung object at Rosslyn; rather, it uses documented motifs (Boaz/Jachin, crypt→choir→apse, Marian devotion, angelic orders) to present a historically informed ascent.

Correspondence method (esoteric layer)

Each step lists: Sphere (sefirotic/planetary), Angel (Shem or Enochian-adjacent), Virtue, and an Artifact anchor (e.g., Apprentice Pillar, Lady Chapel, Musical Cubes). This is a fusion map: historically grounded anchors + clearly labeled interpretive correspondences.

Accessibility

All steps are keyboard reachable (Tab), controls work with Enter/Space. Descriptions are written to support screen readers.

Tip: begin ascent and use the step buttons to reveal plates one by one. Planetary light will tint the page as hours change.
