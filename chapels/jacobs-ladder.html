What this page is (and isn’t)

This is a study visualization that teaches how medieval cosmograms structure ascent. It is not claiming a literal 33‑rung ladder at Rosslyn; rather, it pairs attested motifs (pillars, crypt, Lady Chapel, Green Men, choir) with standard esoteric attributions (sefirot, paths, planets/zodiac, tarot trumps).

Correspondence keys

Sefirot 1–10: common archangel set (Sandalphon, Gabriel, Michael, Haniel, Raphael, Kamael, Tzadkiel, Tzaphkiel, Raziel, Metatron). Paths 11–32: Hebrew letter, GD/Thelemic elemental/planetary/zodiacal attributions and Tarot trumps (Thoth names in parentheses where they differ: e.g., Justice→Adjustment, Temperance→Art, Strength→Lust).
