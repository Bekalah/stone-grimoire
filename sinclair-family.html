Sinclair Patrons

The Sinclair family, hereditary patrons of Rosslyn, commissioned the chapel in the mid-15th century. Their heraldry and esoteric alliances mark the nave as a fusion of knightly, mystical, and artistic currents.

Founders

  • William Sinclair (1410–1484): 1st Earl of Caithness, builder of Rosslyn Chapel.
  • Sinclair Lineage: linked to Scottish nobility, Knights Templar lore, and speculative Masonry.

Symbolism

Heraldic shields appear throughout the chapel. Some scholars see echoes of:

  • Masonic compasses and pillars
  • Templar crosses and Grail lore
  • Qabalistic Tree of Life imagery
