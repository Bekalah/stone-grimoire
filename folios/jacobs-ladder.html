Vision

This ladder rises not in rungs, but in spirals. Like the Apprentice Pillar of Rosslyn, it is a helix woven of 72 names — the Shem ha-Mephorash angels — each a step of sound, color, and archetype. To climb is to shift tone, to cloak yourself in style, to breathe through zodiacal fire and planetary light.

Reflection

In Hermetic measure, the 72 divide the zodiac wheel (360° ÷ 5°). Each angel is a face of the cosmic choir, linked to a planetary current, a solfeggio tone, and a visual skin. By fusing these, the Ladder is not abstract doctrine but living art.
